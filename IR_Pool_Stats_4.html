Group stage — final headlines

Messi rewrote history.

Hat-trick vs Algeria, brace vs Austria, a free-kick off the bench vs Jordan. 6 goals, 3 games, and he's now the all-time leading scorer in World Cup history with 19 career goals — passing Miroslav Klose. Dan Tovar picked him as top scorer. Dan called it.

Colombia topped their group 🇨🇴

A scoreless draw with Portugal in the final group game was enough — Colombia finished first in Group K with 7 points. They now face Ghana in the Round of 32. Isabel and María Camila, your pick is still very much alive.

Mbappé, Haaland, Dembélé, Vinícius — all tied.

Four goals each. Mbappé is now France's all-time record scorer, passing Olivier Giroud. 8 IR picks have Mbappé as top scorer — they're all still firmly in the race behind Messi.

Kane is right there.

3 goals through the group stage, tied with a pack of strikers just behind the leaders. Isabel and María Camila's pick is still alive for the Golden Boot, even if Messi has a big head start.

Big names already out.

Japan, South Africa and Germany were among the first teams eliminated in the Round of 32. Edna — Germany lost to Paraguay. The Germany redemption arc is over for this tournament.

Argentina, France, Mexico: perfect groups.

All three finished the group stage with 9 points from 9 — not a single dropped point. Rafay (Argentina), Zeshan/Isabel/Criz (France), and anyone counting on co-host Mexico are looking very comfortable right now.
