Matchday 1 — what we learned
The Spain situation
6 people picked Spain to win this whole thing. Spain's first game? A 0-0 draw against Cabo Verde. That's right — Cabo Verde. An island nation of 500,000 people. Stamatis, Saad, Gerasimos, María Camila, Dani B., and Alyson: your champion has some explaining to do.
Goals? Germany has plenty.
Germany 7 — Curaçao 1. Seven. Edna, who picked Germany to win the whole tournament, is looking increasingly less crazy. Kai Havertz alone scored twice. Florian Wirtz is putting on a clinic. The rest of us may owe Edna an apology.
Dan Tovar's quiet power move
Dan is the only person who picked Messi as top scorer. Messi then scored a hat-trick in Argentina's first game — his first-ever World Cup hat-trick, at 38 years old. Dan says nothing. Dan just wins.
Portugal: also disappointing
Dan and Cesar both picked Portugal as champion. Portugal drew 1-1 with Congo DR. Not exactly the statement of a team about to win a World Cup. Ronaldo played 90 minutes and scored zero goals. Just like the last World Cup. And the one before that.
France looked sharp
Mbappé scored twice, France won 3-1, and the 3 people who picked France — Zeshan, Isabel, and Criz — are feeling very good about themselves right now. Deservedly so.
USA 4 — Paraguay 1
Folarin Balogun had two goals before halftime. The USA is playing like a team that actually wants to win its own World Cup. Everyone who put USA in their quarterfinalists is sitting comfortably. Stamatis, Dan, Cesar, Alyson: good call.
